Where Colors Collide: Finding Home in the Multi Identity Art Collective.

UCSD's all-embracing art collective, MIAC, fosters a space where artists of all backgrounds can connect and create.

INCLUSIVITY

Origins of the Multi–Identity Art Collective

In 2022, the Multi-Identity Art Collective (MIAC) was founded to create a warm and inclusive space for artists at UCSD. I had the pleasure of collaborating with MIAC to develop their brand identity and design their website.

MARKETABILITY

Who's invited?

Yusuf Adebayo Grillo posed a thought-provoking question: "When Does Art Become Science and Science Art?" His answer? "Never." The distinction between art and science remains clear, with each realm tending to operate independently. This separation is also evident in social circles, as STEM individuals often gravitate towards one another.

At UCSD, where a majority of students are STEM scholars, a crucial question arises: How can the Multi-Identity Art Collective (MIAC) effectively market itself as a welcoming and inclusive organization for all students?

BRAND IDENTITY

Developing their presence

To foster a sense of inclusivity at the Multi-Identity Art Collective (MIAC), I developed a style guide that effectively communicates their message to the UCSD community. This guide highlights MIAC's commitment to welcoming everyone, regardless of artistic skill level or background. The mascot and signature orange and blue color scheme further reinforce their image as a vibrant and inclusive space.

EVENTS

Check us out!

Bridging the gap: At MIAC, I designed a user-friendly events page that integrates with calendars like Google Calendar. This empowered students to stay informed and participate in the art community.

ABOUT US

Learn about us

Building trust within the community relies heavily on fostering a human connection. Recognizing this, we went beyond simply showcasing MIAC's welcoming mascot on the website. We incorporated visuals featuring the passionate individuals who are the heart and soul of the organization.

SEE YOU THERE

Come join us!

Not everyone connects with art in the same way, but the need for belonging is a universal human truth. Through design, I strive to create welcoming and engaging experiences for every visitor, fostering a sense of connection. Imagine these visitors transforming into active members, sharing their positive experiences and building a vibrant community through events, workshops, and collaborations. This vision fuels my passion to keep designing – to be the bridge that connects individuals and fosters a sense of belonging.
