Leveling the Playing Field for Education.

Empowering students to reach their full potential, Inspirame's innovative technology delivers personalized guidance on higher ed and careers. It acts as a vital support system for low-income and first-generation students who may not have access to a traditional mentor.

OVERVIEW

How can we consolidate our design system?

At Inspirame, I helped shape the future of U.S. higher education by streamlining their core products. I unified the design language and developed data management systems for TecoAdmin, their college and career guidance platform for administrators. This ensures a seamless user experience for educators, empowering them to better support students.

DATA REPORT

Empower decisions through data

A stark divide exists in California's education system: while Latinos make up nearly 40% of the population, only 20% hold Bachelor's degrees. Similarly, 60% of Black high school graduates lack the A-G requirements for universities. This data underscores the urgent need for solutions.

TecoAdmin empowers organizations with robust data analytics, enabling informed decision-making. I played a crucial role in introducing this functionality and designing compelling data visualizations. These insights pave the way for a more equitable educational landscape, particularly for underserved communities like the Latino population in California.

DESIGN CHOICES

Setting the foundation for generations to come

Making a brand that grows not only with time, but with the company and our values was daunting. Does this component work all across the product? What principles do I base my choices around? Some principles that I defined included responsiveness, accessibility, and scannability.

RESPONSIVENESS

Responsive layouts demand adaptable information spaces. We define general spacing for tables in specific areas, while allowing for data-driven adjustments based on user needs and available screen space.

ACCESSIBILITY

Data is visualized for easy comprehension, with customizable light and dark mode themes empowering users to personalize their viewing experience.

SCANNABILITY

Streamlining user experience: Clear headings and ample spacing within forms minimize cognitive load, preventing users from feeling overwhelmed by information overload.
