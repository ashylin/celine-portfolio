ESPORTS DEVELOPMENT

The Road to Pro: Streamlining the Journey

Many gamers can play competitive games- with some aiming to leave the casual scene and develop as professional gamers.FACEIT bridges this gap and connects competitive players to the Esports scene. I worked on the client interface to ease the career journey for professional-seeking individuals.

COMPETITIVE SCENE

FACEIT Major: London Takes Center Stage

In the year 2018, Valve, the developers of the Counter-Strike series, announced that FACEIT would host the thirteenth Global Offensive major. It was the first Majojr hosted by FACEIT and the first hosted in the United Kingdom. Since then, FACEIT has consolidated itself as the Esports platform to use, and I developed the new client interface for the app.

BRAND IDENTITY

Building a New Brand Identity

Creating a brand that elevates and expands what it means to be a professional in their craft is harder than it looks. It involves thinking about how design elements come together to create the impact that you want. Some elements that I redesigned involved the font and shape of the iconic orange cursor to set it apart from just being a recolored cursor and establish more of an identity.

ADAPTIVE LAYOUTS

Grid Navigation System

When redesigned an interface that contains many of features, we want to make sure that the features usage rates are high. The new iteration divides all of the features across two spaces, reducing the amount of screen switching a user will have to do and allows for lesser used features like Shop to gain more traction.

NEWS AND ARTICLES BOARD

News and Updates

As Esports can be difficult to stay up-to-date with, a new feature called News was introduced. I included a space were articles varying from simple game updates to the current state of Esports and possible openings for teams can be viewed. This consolidates their whole journey into the industry all in one space, with resources that they can use to help guide them.

GAME OVER

Let's party up!

The world of esports and gaming transcends professional aspirations. It's a vibrant community where passionate individuals, from casual players to aspiring pros, connect and share their love for the game. This shared passion fuels my desire to design spaces that foster strong communities – inclusive environments where everyone feels welcome to compete, collaborate, and celebrate their love for gaming.
